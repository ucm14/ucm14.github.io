Author: Minfeng "Mason" Yu Date: November 17, 2024  19:35:04 Category: -Music

The second mini album of XG, “AWE”, was dropped on 11.8.2024. To be honest, it is not as amazing as their first mini album “New DNA”, which earned many attention due to its extremely high music quality and cutting-edge concept design. However, “AWE” still an outstanding album comparing other pop music album in 2024. The album contains 6 tracks: “Howl“ as an intro, the title song “Howling“, the skit, two pre-released song “IYKYK“ and “Somethin’ ain’t right“ and two unexpected R&B song “In The Rain“ “Is This Love“. The remix of “woke up“ was added in the album, which is a confusing decision.
