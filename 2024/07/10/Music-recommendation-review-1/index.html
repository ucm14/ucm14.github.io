A shooting star in pop music industry

XG (acronym for Xtraordinary Girls) is a Japanese hip-hop & RnB girl group belongs to AVEX but based in South Korea, which has 7 members (the eldest is born in 2002 and the youngest born in 2005). Names sorted by age from oldest to youngest are as follow: Chisa, Hinata, Jurin, Harvey, Julia, Maya, Cocona. They made their debut on March 18, 2022, with the release of the digital single “Tippy Toes”.

XG (From left to right, they are Hinata, Jurin, Julia, Cocona, Maya, Chisa, Harvey)

On March 18, 2022, XG made their debut with the release of their all English-language digital single “Tippy Toes”. On June 29, they released their second all English-language single titled “Mascara”, followed by their first television appearance on South Korean Mnet’s M Countdown performing the single. On November 17, XG released a rap cypher video entitled Galz Xypher (Galz Xypher#2). The video became viral on a TikTok through an XG fan account, gaining over 15 million views on the platform.

On January 25, 2023, XG released their third all English-language single, “Shooting Star” along with its music video and an additional track, “Left Right”. On March 6, XG entered the Media base US Radio Top 40, becoming the first female Japanese artist and the first Japanese group to do so. On June 30, XG released “Grl Gvng” accompanied by its music video, as a pre-release single preceding their debut EP New DNA. On August 4, XG released “TGIF” accompanied by its music video, as the second pre-release single to their debut EP New DNA. On August 23, XG released “New Dance” accompanied by its music video, as the third and final pre-release single to their debut EP New DNA. On September 27, XG’s debut EP New DNA was released alongside its lead single “Puppet Show“, accompanied by its music video. The EP was certified gold by the Recording Industry Association of Japan (RIAJ) for shipments of over 100,000 copies.

XG’s first surge in popularity was due to “Shooting Star” and “Left Right”. Even though they already posted 2 singles, their make-ups and concepts were similar to other KPOP groups. In other words, they had not discovered their own unique characteristics. However, in “Shooting Star” and “Left Right”, the music perfectly struck a chord with the audience’s hearts while embracing the millennial retro trend. To me, another reason that can’t ignore is there lyrics are English, which is more easier for audience to listen, remember and perform. Personally, I prefer left right to shooting star, the former one is a typical RnB which fits my bias really well.

A Cut of "Left Right"

The lyrics in “Shooting Star” vividly depicted their trainee experience: “Always lookin’ fresh, got that green in my diet”, “Everything I do, I do it A1, flawless” sang by Jurin; “Babe, if I give it my all, will it pay off? Workin’ overtime, no days off” by Chisa; “Now I’m goin’ up, headin’ to the stars. Wouldn’t trade it out for another life” by Julia. These lyrics also perfectly showcase the team’s efforts to achieve their dreams and their pride of being one of the team. In past 7 years, they devoted countless effort in practice, encountered with numerous psychological and life challenges, as well as a brutal elimination process. Now, these seven wolves can stand on the spotlighted stage and calmly yet proudly singing:“It’s a big move, every day’s like a dream, Makin’ big moves, yeah, we do it for our team, Ooh, ah, I’m lookin’ so lavish, Ooh, ah, yeah, I bet you can’t imagine.” What’s more, the dance performance can kill every KPOP girl group. Since I know a little about dance, I recommend this video for better understanding of their dance and why they outperform other peers.

“New DNA” was a bold and successful endeavor on the integration of concept and musicality together with visual performance. Before this, the continuity of the concept and the diversity of the music in this album were unimaginable. I am going to explain this point of view from the storyline and soundtracks.

Concept photo of Girl Gang

We have to start with the intro, “Hesonoo”. In Japanese, it means “umbilical cord”. It is also the slogan of the group, representing their their closely connected lives and emotions. Next up is the song “x-gene”, the two songs symbolize the awakening of extraterrestrial beings and the closely-knit team relationship; “Puppet Show” tells the story of them breaking free from their restraints; “Grl Gvng” is more like a superhero movie. This song intends to express their own incomparable strength and issue a severe warning to their enemies; “TGIF” and “New Dance” tells the story of the experiences and encounters of seven extraterrestrials after landing on Earth. Overall, it revolves around the theme of female self-awareness awakening, while blending in the typical hard-edged style of hip-hop. Truly, those girl groups, IVE, LE SSERAFIM, GIDLE are all playing such thing. However, it seems lack of credibility when those girls dressed in revealing outfits, with altered faces, performing lackluster dances, sing songs telling women not to care about others’ gazes, all I know is they are playing with females’ trust and trying to make genuine money by relying on these hollow elements. Their music production and choreography are outstanding as well. We can hear drill hi-hat clicking in the embryo, we can feel the drum shaking from the puppet world, we can dance to the bass blended in new dance. I deeply believe many people who were new to XG became fans of them through the “POP POP POP” section made by Harvey and I am one of them. New Dance gives me a totally different feel when comparing to other music in the album. I can find chill and relax in this song, I can rest my body but move my heartbeat with their dance steps. A cool and sweet soda definitely be much more popular than Jack Daniels in a hot summer.

The sickest shot I've ever seen in my life

“Woke Up” is A true revolution in the history of girl group. It is so hard to imagine that an entire rap song performed by a girl group and the visionary is so fierce, strong, and impressing. overlook the astonishing details and seamless scene transitions hidden in the MV but focus on what these girls doing because it is impossible for me to find and explain every one of them. We can ignore Jurin rapping in fire and the “Tokyo drift” bar, we can brush off Maya’s machinegun-like flow, we can pass over Harvey drop from outer space and reveal her true self in the midst of the flames, we can overlook the sick eye and catchy flow made by Hinata and Julia, we can set aside Chisa’s captivating melodic rap. But, we can not take no notice off what Cocona does, To me, that is the soul of this song. You can say she shaved her hair in music video is for attention and clout, you also can blame the producer for asking her give up her black hair and scoff at the announcement that she wanted to do it herself. Nevertheless, this has already become A FACT. SHE ACTUALLY DID THIS AND NOT JUST MAKE IT EXISTS IN TALKS. She has left many people shocked and in awe of what she has done.

After all, it is their 100% dedication to practice, musical diversity, and perfect teamwork that garnered them much attention and love around the globe. The XG MOVE, XG VOX or XG XYPHER all showcased their professionalism, hard work, and talent in performance, as well as their ease in mastering various genres. Tippy toes and Mascara both too Korean and can not help them establish a solid foothold in the music market which full of competence. Luckily, they find out the optimal method to express themselves and continuously striving forward on this path. Therefore, their success is well-deserved. But to me, I believe they still have not touched the ceiling. Such a valuable team deserves to enjoy larger stages, sharing their musical vision, stories and charm with a broader audience.
