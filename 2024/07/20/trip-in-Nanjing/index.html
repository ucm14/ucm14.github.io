Trip in Nanjing

Posted on 2024-07-20 Edited on 2024-07-28 In Travel

What we did in the weekend?

Basically, we didn’t decide to take outdoor activities at daytime (even at nighttime) due to the extreme hot climate. Thus, we turned out vision to indoor activities. And, to me, Nanjing is a city which has enormous delicious food, so I took my friends to have those splendid dishes, mainly.

1. Noodle

Recently, Nanjing’s noodle is very popular not only for the astonishing appearance (a huge pot-stewed meat) but also for its complex and amazing taste. We went to a noodle shop near Nanjing University (actually, Southeast University and Nanjing University both at same street) which is famous due to the large meatball where a salted yolk hide in it.

You can see from the picture, this costed me 46 yuan, which is quite expensive. However, the taste matched its price. It is too delicious that I have difficulty finding proper words to precisely describe it.

2. Duck

There’s a saying that roasted duck in Nanjing is the origin of Beijing roasted duck. I am 100% willing to believe that be a truth. The reason is simple: I don’t like the roasted duck being too greasy, and Nanjing roasted duck just at the balance point of fatty and dry. The soul of duck is the sweet- and-salty sauce. Dipping a piece of duck in the sauce can improve the taste and make the duck meat becomes much more tender. Sadly, my friends seemed not a fan of it. They preferred steamed rice roll.

3. Dishes

We went to the top restaurant in Nanjing. Cuisines here are more sweeter than other places. The restaurant become crowded at 11 am which is truly scare to see.
